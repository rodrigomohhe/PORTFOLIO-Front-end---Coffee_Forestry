Our coffee, our commitment to sustainability.

At Coffee Forestry, we're committed to creating a better world through our coffee. By sourcing our beans exclusively from agroforestry systems and working directly with small peasant farmers, we're able to ensure that our coffee is not only delicious but also sustainable. By supporting these farmers and promoting sustainable farming practices, we're able to make a difference in the coffee industry and beyond.

What is Coffee Agroforestry? And what benefits does it bring to the planet?

Article by Exemple Woodnash dos Santos

Agroforestry provides numerous benefits for the environment, including reducing soil erosion, improving soil health, increasing biodiversity, and sequestering carbon from the atmosphere. It also promotes sustainable land use practices that can help combat climate change and support the overall health and well-being of ecosystems.

Revolutionizing Agriculture: How Agroforestry Can Change Our World

Article by Exemple Woodnash dos Santos

Sustainable coffee production can contribute to community development by providing jobs, supporting local economies, and promoting social and environmental responsibility. Providing equal opportunities for women and men to participate, from farming and processing to management and leadership roles. Helping to ensure that women have access to resources, education, and decision-making power, creating a more equitable and diverse industry.
